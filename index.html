Knife Information

Our knife products contain less impurities and are of higher grade.

We begin our knife levels at Shirogami (White steel No. 2) and end at Supa-aogami (Super blue steel) to guarantee you the highest quality.

Shirogami (White steel) knives will: rust less and need less care, but will require more sharpening.

Aogami (Blue steel) knives will: rust more easily and need more care, but will requre less sharpening and are most durable.

All of our knives use Kurumi (Walnut) wood handles with plastic or buffalo horn caps.

Our knife steel polished choices come in 1. Tosa Style (black non-polished) or 2. Polished.
